Features

Our app uses advanced machine learning algorithms to monitor your child's online activity and alert you to potential dangers, including cyberbullying, sexting, depression, and exposure to inappropriate content.

  • Real-time monitoring
  • Multi-platform support
  • Parental control tools
  • Weekly activity summary

How it Works

Our app scans your child's online activity on platforms such as text messages, email, social media, and school-issued accounts. It searches for keywords and phrases that may indicate a potential problem, and sends notifications to you if any are detected.

Benefits

  • Protect your child from online dangers
  • Stay informed about your child's online activity
  • Set screen time limits and restrict access to inappropriate websites and apps
  • Peace of mind knowing your child is safe online
