About

The internet can be a great resource for children, but it's important to keep them safe. This website provides information and resources to help parents keep their children safe online.

Tips

  • Keep the computer in a common area, not in your child's bedroom
  • Monitor your child's internet use
  • Teach your child not to share personal information online
  • Use parental controls to block inappropriate contentRegularly check your child's internet history and social media accounts
  • Educate your child about online dangers and cyberbullying
  • Set clear rules and consequences for breaking them
  • Stay informed about new technology and the websites your child is using
