Introduction

The internet is an amazing resource, but it can also be a dangerous place for children. In this site, we will discuss the risks and dangers that children face on the internet and provide tips for keeping children safe online.

Risks and Dangers

There are many risks and dangers that children face on the internet, including cyberbullying, exposure to inappropriate content, and online predators. It's important for parents and guardians to be aware of these risks and take steps to protect their children.

Prevention Tips

  • Monitor your child's online activity and set guidelines for their internet use
  • Teach your child about online safety, including how to identify and avoid dangers
  • Keep the computer in a public area of your home and limit the amount of time your child spends online
  • Install filters and parental control software to block inappropriate content
